# e-plantShopping

Welcome to **e-plantShopping**, your ultimate online destination for bringing nature closer to your home or office. Whether you are a seasoned plant parent or just starting your green journey, our platform makes discovering and purchasing the perfect plants seamless and enjoyable.

---

## 🌿 Project Overview

**e-plantShopping** is a modern e-commerce web application designed to simplify the process of buying plants online. The platform provides users with an intuitive interface to browse through a diverse catalog of indoor, outdoor, and exotic plants, learn about their care requirements, and securely purchase them.

### Key Features
* **Curated Plant Catalog:** Browse plants by categories (e.g., Low Light, Air Purifying, Pet-Friendly, Succulents).
* **Detailed Plant Profiles:** Access essential care tips, watering schedules, and sunlight requirements for every plant.
* **Intuitive Shopping Cart:** Easily add, remove, and manage your selected greenery before checking out.
* **Responsive Design:** A seamless shopping experience across mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.
